How it works

Set your age, height, weight, activity level, and nutrition goals. DietPlanner calculates daily targets using established formulas and generates meal suggestions from public nutrition datasets — no account, no cloud, no tracking.

1. Set your profile Enter basic body metrics and choose a goal — lose fat, gain muscle, maintain, or eat balanced.
2. Get your plan Receive daily calorie and macro targets with meal suggestions tailored to your preferences.
3. Explore food Search a bundled nutrition database from public-source references (USDA, NIH, WHO).

Premium features

AI Chef Pro 30-day rotating meal calendars with adaptive grocery lists that match your plan.
Deep Food Intel Detailed food-vs-goal reports: nutrient breakdown, cautions, and smart substitutions.

Premium features are digital functionality delivered inside the app after blockchain confirmation. Feature durations (30-day, one-time, 24-hour) are shown before purchase. Blockchain transactions are public and generally irreversible.
