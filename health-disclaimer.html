Health Disclaimer

DietPlanner is not health advice.

DietPlanner provides general nutrition education and meal planning suggestions only. It is not intended to be, and must not be used as, a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, cure, or disease prevention.

Consult a professional

Always consult a qualified physician, registered dietitian, or healthcare provider before making dietary changes, especially if you have diabetes, kidney disease, cardiovascular disease, food allergies, eating disorders, gastrointestinal conditions, are pregnant or nursing, are underweight, are an athlete with specialized needs, or take medication that interacts with food.

Estimates and limitations

Calorie and macronutrient targets are estimates generated from user inputs and generalized formulas such as Mifflin-St Jeor. Individual metabolism, medical needs, medications, food preparation, brands, and nutrient bioavailability vary significantly.

No guarantee

DietPlanner does not guarantee weight loss, weight gain, improved appearance, disease prevention, performance improvement, or any other health outcome.

Emergency and medical care

Do not use DietPlanner for urgent medical decisions. If you believe you may have a medical emergency, contact emergency services immediately.
