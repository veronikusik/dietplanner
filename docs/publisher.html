What we build

Native Android dApps for the Solana Mobile platform. Our applications follow three guiding principles:

  • Local-first data. Personal profile information is stored on the user's device using OS-level secure storage. We do not operate account servers, do not collect telemetry, and do not embed analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • Self-custodial payments. Premium feature unlocks are paid for through the user's own Solana wallet via the Mobile Wallet Adapter standard. We do not custody funds, private keys, recovery phrases, or any wallet credentials.
  • Transparent consent. Every on-chain transaction is preceded by an in-app confirmation modal showing the exact amount, the recipient treasury wallet, and the irreversibility of the transfer. Users must explicitly opt in to each purchase before any wallet popup appears.
